Hindu Samajotsava is a festival that celebrates our culture, strength and unity. It is a platform to remember our roots and build the future of a strong India. A festival that proclaims the unity of Hindu society with the enthusiasm of youth power. A special occasion that is the confluence of heritage and progress. Let us participate with our family and friends in this festival that aims to bring about transformation in Hindu society through social harmony, family education, environmental protection, indigenous lifestyle and civic etiquette.
